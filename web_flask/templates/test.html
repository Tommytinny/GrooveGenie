GrooveGenie

"Music has always been a huge part of my life. I’ve spent countless hours curating playlists, searching for new artists, and sharing recommendations with friends. I wanted to create something that would simplify this process—a tool that could understand musical preferences and help others discover music the way I do, without the endless search. This project became more than just a portfolio piece; it was my way of blending my passion for music with my skills as a developer."
